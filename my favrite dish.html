Biryani Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Basmati rice
  • 500g Chicken or Mutton
  • 1 cup Yogurt
  • 2 large Onions (sliced)
  • 2 Tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1 tablespoon Ginger-Garlic paste
  • 1 tablespoon Biryani masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon Red chili powder
  • Fresh Coriander and Mint leaves
  • Salt to taste
  • 4 tablespoons Oil or Ghee
  • Saffron or Yellow food color (optional)

Instructions

  1. Wash and soak the rice for 30 minutes. Boil it until it's 70% cooked and drain the water.
  2. Marinate the chicken or mutton with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, turmeric, chili powder, biryani masala, and salt. Let it rest for 1 hour.
  3. Heat oil in a pan and fry the sliced onions until golden brown. Remove half for garnishing.
  4. Add chopped tomatoes and marinated meat to the remaining onions. Cook until the meat is tender and oil separates.
  5. In a large pot, layer rice and meat mixture. Sprinkle chopped mint, coriander leaves, and fried onions between layers.
  6. Mix saffron or food color with 2 tablespoons of milk and pour over the top layer of rice (optional).

  8. Turn off the heat and let it rest for 10 minutes before serving.

Serve hot with raita, salad, or boiled eggs. Enjoy your delicious homemade biryani!
